# Authors

Flyva is built and used day to day by the team at **[Owls Department](https://www.owlsdepartment.com)**.

Owls Department is an award-recognized, technology-led digital studio, rooted in creative development and digital design. Building our careers on the open web, we are committed to contributing back by sharing useful tools that we build for ourselves.

## Team
